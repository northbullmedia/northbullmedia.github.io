Founders

North Bull Media was founded by two brothers from Jaipur, India — Ujjwal Soni and Rudraksh Soni. Their journey began in January 2023 when they launched an automotive Instagram page that would later evolve into a multi-platform media ecosystem.

Ujjwal Soni started building Cars35 while pursuing his BBA at St. Xavier’s College, Jaipur. Rudraksh Soni began working on Cars35 at just 15, showing an early obsession with digital content strategy, automotive culture, and platform-building.

The company faced major setbacks when multiple high-growth pages were suspended due to fake copyright strikes. Instead of stopping, the founders rebuilt everything from scratch — proving resilience, speed of execution, and long-term vision.

Today, North Bull Media continues to expand aggressively with new verticals, long-form YouTube content ambitions, and the launch of Bikes35 as a major platform for India’s motorcycle community.
